Down the River Company

Our mission is to bring joy, adventure, and unforgettable experiences to all our clients. We believe in teamwork, safety, and fun on every rafting trip.

History

It all began with a small group of friends who spent their weekends rafting down local rivers with nothing more than an old inflatable boat and a shared love for adventure. The thrill of navigating rapids, the laughter when someone fell in, and the quiet moments drifting under the sun created memories they couldn't stop talking about.

What started as a hobby grew into a vision. Pooling their savings, they bought proper gear, secured permits, and founded Down the River, a company built on friendship and passion. Today, it has become a trusted name in rafting adventures, offering trips that carry forward the same spirit of joy, teamwork, and respect for nature that inspired those first journeys.
